Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of saris are available in your collection?


Our sari collection includes a diverse range of styles, fabrics, and designs. You can explore traditional silk saris, modern georgette saris, and everything in between. We strive to offer a wide variety to cater to different tastes and occasions.

2. How do I choose the right size for my sari?


Saris are generally one-size-fits-all garments, and the length can vary. However, we provide detailed product descriptions, including dimensions, to help you make an informed decision. If you have specific size concerns, feel free to reach out to our customer support for personalized assistance.

3. Can I return or exchange a sari if it doesn't meet my expectations?


Yes, we have a hassle-free return and exchange policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the sari within a specified timeframe for a refund or exchange. Please review our return policy for more details and steps on how to initiate a return.

4. Are the colors of the saris accurately represented on the website?


We make every effort to display the colors of our saris as accurately as possible. However, please note that colors may vary slightly due to different monitor settings. If you have specific color concerns, you can contact our customer support for additional information or request fabric swatches.

5. Do you offer customization options for the saris, such as blouse stitching or additional embellishments?


Currently, we do not offer customization services on our platform. However, we are continuously working to enhance our services, and we appreciate your feedback. If you have specific customization requests, feel free to share them with us, and we will consider them for future improvements.

6. How do I care for my sari to ensure its longevity?


Each sari comes with care instructions, which typically include information on washing, ironing, and storing the garment. We recommend following these guidelines to preserve the beauty and quality of your sari. If you have any additional questions about maintenance, our customer support team is available to assist you.
